Structural Bioinformatics

Using BioPython, I parsed PDB crystal structures to quantify the "environmental noise" caused by the SOD1-G93A mutation.

  • Input: PDB File (2NNX)
  • Algorithm: RMSD & Anisotropy calculation.
  • Output: Noise Parameters ($D_{noise}$) for the quantum Hamiltonian.

Quantum Dynamics (Lindblad)

The ion channel is modeled as an Open Quantum System. The G93A mutation acts as a noise generator in the Hamiltonian.

  • Engine: QuTiP (Quantum Toolbox in Python).
  • Equation: $\dot{\rho} = -i[H, \rho] + \mathcal{L}(\rho)$
  • Result: 62.0% Decrease in Coherence Time.

Neuromorphic Simulation

Bridging the gap to physiology. The collapse of the quantum wave function is translated into reduced ion conductance.

  • Engine: Brian2 Spiking Neural Network.
  • Metric: Action Potential Latency.
  • Validation: Significant delay observed ($p < 0.001$).
